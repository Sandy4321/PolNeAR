Breitbart News Daily: A World at War.

On the November 24, Breitbart News Daily show on Sirius XM Patriot channel 125 from 6AM to 9AM EST, host and Breitbart News Executive Chairman Stephen K. Bannon will interview a number of guests. The program is the first live, conservative radio enterprise to air seven days a week. Sirius XM Vice President for news and talk Dave Gorab called the show “the conservative news show of record.”

Donald Trump’s campaign manager, Corey Lewandowski, will be interviewed about Trump being under fire about recent comments he made regarding Muslims.

Bannon will interview Col. Rob Maness about the Louisiana governor race results.

Bannon will interview Breitbart News contributor Pamela Geller about the fight against radical Islam.

Author Roger Stone will discuss his new book, The Clinton’s War on Women, and Hillary Clinton’s presidential campaign.

Breitbart Jerusalem editor Aaron Klein will discuss an overlooked Benghazi connection to a recent Mali hotel assault.

Breitbart London’s Raheem Kassam will call in LIVE from London to discuss the Syrian migrant crisis in Europe.

Breitbart Texas editor Brandon Darby will come on the program to talk about the Southern border and how many Syrians can easily slip through.

Bannon will interview Breitbart News contributor Pamela Geller about the fight against radical Islam.

Breitbart News’ Dr. Thomas Williams will be interviewed about Pope Francis calling Christmas a sham.

The Media Research Center’s Dan Gainor will be interviewed about ABC’s TV show Scandal showing an on-air abortion to the tune of “Silent Night.”

Investigative reporter Julia Hahn will come on the show to talk about immigration and how it’s transforming the country’s demographics and culture.
