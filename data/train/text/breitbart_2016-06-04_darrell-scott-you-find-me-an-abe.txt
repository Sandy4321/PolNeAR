Darrell Scott: ‘You Find Me an Aberration Because I’m Black and I Support Trump!’

From Josh Feldman writing at Mediaite:

"A CNN segment tonight on Donald Trump‘s racially-loaded remarks devolved into a shouting match over black supporters of the presumptive GOP nominee.

"Pastor Darrell Scott defended Trump’s “Look at my African-American over here!” line by saying it was a “term of endearment” and part of Trump’s willingness to be politically incorrect.

"CNN’s Tara Setmayer ridiculed that assessment as “political Stockholm Syndrome” and said, “He felt the need to have to point him out because he was black and in the crowd.”
