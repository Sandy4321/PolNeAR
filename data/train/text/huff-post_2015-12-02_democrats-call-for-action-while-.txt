2016 Democrats Call For Action While Republicans Tweet Prayers After San Bernardino Shooting.

WASHINGTON — U.S. presidential candidates on Wednesday reacted to a mass shooting in San Bernardino, California, where authorities reported at least 20 victims at a nonprofit center that works with individuals with developmental disabilities.

<tweet pic="" author="HillaryClinton" timestamp="1449075600" tweet_id="672149874046083072">I refuse to accept this as normal. We must take action to stop gun violence now. -H https://t.co/SkKglwQycb</tweet>.
